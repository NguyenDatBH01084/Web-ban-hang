TEA STORY

The bubble tea wave has landed and spread to many places around the world. Bubble tea has become a popular "national drink" in Europe and America, bringing the cultural identity of East Asians around the world.

While milk is just an ingredient that creates flavor, tea leaves contain the essence of thousands of years. For Asians, tea is not just a drink, but it also contains the essence of thousands of years of history.

Hanoi milk tea is a favorite drink of young people and an indispensable culinary culture symbol when traveling to Hanoi.
